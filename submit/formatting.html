Formatting Instructions

Name

A reasonably descriptive identifier for the event, 30 or fewer characters. Numbers, letters, and spaces only.

Location

(latitude, longitude) enter both as decimals, degrees north and west.

Date

m/d/y use Gregorian calendar, -y for BC, default day: January 1. 15th of February, 256 BC => 2/15/-256

Type

The manner of causation from one event to another, one of political, ideological.
